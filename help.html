SteamBuddy Help & Features

Welcome to SteamBuddy! This extension enhances your Steam experience by automating redirects and showing review summaries.

Open in Steam Client (Redirect)

Automatically redirects Steam Store links to open directly in the Steam client.

  • Works on game pages like https://store.steampowered.com/app/12345/.
  • Handles PatchBot redirects.
  • Toggle: Enable/disable via the main switch in the popup.
  • Delay: 2-second buffer to ensure page loads before redirect.

Tip: If Steam isn't running, it will launch the client.

Auto Age Verification

Bypasses Steam's age gate by auto-filling a birthdate (18+ years ago).

  • Triggers on agecheck pages.
  • Sets: Day=1, Month=January, Year=Current Year - 18.
  • Always on when redirect is enabled.

Note: For convenience only; ensure you're of legal age.

Ratings Badge (Steam Reviews)

Displays a purple badge below the game title showing Steam review summary.

  • Fetches recent reviews via Steam API.
  • Shows: Positive %, score description, total reviews.
  • Toggle in popup; reloads Steam tabs on change.

Fallback: "Not available" if data can't be loaded.

Check for Free Games & DLCs

One-click button to open Steam's free games/search page.

  • URL: https://store.steampowered.com/search?maxprice=free&specials=1&ndl=1.
  • Opens in new tab.

Troubleshooting

  • Redirects not working? Ensure Steam client is installed and toggle is ON.
  • Badges not showing? Reload Steam tabs or check console (F12).
  • Update: Toggle review badge to reload tabs.
